How to Play

Taking Penalties

1. Click and hold to charge your shot power

2. The red dot will move horizontally - this controls direction

3. Hold longer for higher shots, shorter for lower shots

4. Release to shoot - aim for the corners!

Saving Penalties

1. Move your cursor to position the goalkeeper

2. Click and hold to prepare a dive

3. Release to dive in that direction

4. Try to anticipate where the opponent will shoot!
